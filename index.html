The Studio

Atelier Alma is a Los Angeles ceramic studio rooted in tactile craft, feminine energy, and the kind of art that stays with you. We build hands-on experiences that blur the line between festival activation and intimate workshop — always with clay, always with intention.

The Coachella
Activation
Sip.   Create.   Take Home.

Guests walk into the Sip Margs footprint and walk out with something real — a ceramic piece they made with their own hands. Mini margarita charms. Painted lighter casings. Trinket dishes stamped with the Sip Margs logo. Clay they sculpted while sipping the best marg of their weekend. It's not a booth. It's a memory.

01
Signature Charm Workshop

Hand-sculpt mini margarita charms, lime slices, and cactus pieces. Air-dry clay, glazed and branded. The ultimate festival keepsake.

02
Ceramic Lighter Casings

Paint your own ceramic lighter casing on-site. Take it home that day. Every one unique. Every one Sip Margs branded on the base.

03
Trinket Dish Studio

Craft a custom trinket dish stamped with Sip Margs decals and personalized with your own touch. Small enough to carry out. Big enough to remember forever.

04
Clay Sculpting Experience

Our premium tier. Full sculpting session with an Atelier Alma artist, kiln-fired after the festival, and shipped directly to your door. Coachella in your home forever.
