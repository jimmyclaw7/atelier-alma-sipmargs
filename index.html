At Coachella, guests sip Sip Margs while handcrafting their own take-home ceramic piece — a custom Sip Margs moment, made by them, forever theirs.


Two brand personalities. One unforgettable experience. When people make something with their hands, they remember it forever. That's the power of ceramic activation.

03 — The Experiences

What guests create

🍋
Signature Charm Workshop

Guests paint pre-made Sip Margs ceramic charms: mini margarita glasses, lime slices, salt-rimmed cups, cactus, and "SIP MORE" script pieces. Each charm is a wearable Coachella memory.

🪨
Ceramic Lighter Casings

Guests paint custom Sip Margs ceramic lighter casings — functional art they actually use. Every time they spark it, they remember the moment.

🫙
Trinket Dish & Coaster Studio

Pre-made trinket dishes and coasters stamped with Sip Margs branding. Guests personalize with color, pattern, and texture. Ready to take home or ship.

🤲
Clay Sculpting Workshop

For a more immersive experience: guests hand-build their own pieces from raw clay. Atelier Alma fires and ships finished pieces post-Coachella — no mess, all magic.
