The Activation

Charm Workshop

Guests paint custom Sip Margs ceramic charms: mini margarita glasses with a salt rim, lime wedge slices, a tiny cactus in a terracotta pot, or a SIP MORE banner. Every charm different. Every charm Instagrammable.

Lighter Casings

Custom ceramic lighter covers painted on the spot. Functional, personal, and impossible to leave behind. They go home. They end up on every flat surface. They start conversations.

Trinket Dishes

Pre-made dishes stamped with Sip Margs branding. Guests add color and personality. Done in minutes, remembered forever. Every desk and dresser needs one.

PREMIUM TIER

Clay Sculpting

Guests hand-build from scratch. Atelier Alma fires and ships the finished piece after Coachella. The ultimate keepsake. The only activation guests are still talking about six months later.
