Meet Natalie Valencia

Natalie Valencia is a Miami-based artist whose work has been shown at Art Basel Miami and Art Basel Switzerland. That same fine-art sensibility is the foundation of Atelier Alma, her ceramic events company. She runs intimate, hands-on workshops in Miami and Los Angeles where guests create real ceramic pieces to take home.

She has collaborated with studios and brands in LA. Her setup is fully portable. No warehouse. No logistics headaches. Just art, energy, and guests who leave with something real.

The Activation

Charm Workshop

Guests paint Sip Margs ceramic charms: mini margarita glasses with a salt rim, lime wedge slices, a tiny cactus in a terracotta pot, or a yellow SIP MORE banner. Every charm different. Every charm Instagrammable.

Lighter Casings

Custom ceramic lighter covers painted on the spot. Functional, personal, and impossible to leave behind.

Trinket Dishes

Pre-made dishes stamped with Sip Margs branding. Guests add color and personality. Done in minutes, remembered forever.

PREMIUM TIER

Clay Sculpting

Guests hand-build from scratch. Atelier Alma fires and ships the finished piece after Coachella. The ultimate keepsake.

Let's Make It Happen
